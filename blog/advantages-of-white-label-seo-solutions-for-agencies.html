The Strategic Advantages of White Label SEO Solutions for Agencies

SEO Guides
SUSO 28 February 2024

Find out how agencies can benefit from partnering with a specialist white label SEO agency.

Author: Chetan Bhatt, Marketing Manager

 

 

 

 

 

 

Every brand jostles for the coveted top SERP spots, vying for the eyes and clicks that translate to profit. In this relentless pursuit, agencies face a constant pressure to outmanoeuvre and stay ahead of the curve. The competition has grown fierce, with search algorithms evolving at lightning speed and user expectations soaring ever higher.

While building in-house expertise for every facet of SEO is a valuable pursuit, it can be resource-intensive and time-consuming. This is where White Label SEO emerges as a strategic game-changer. It’s your secret weapon, allowing you to seamlessly integrate external, expert-driven SEO solutions under your own brand.

Here’s a brief overview of key advantages that finding a white label SEO partner brings:

  • Client Satisfaction: Offer a full spectrum of SEO services, encompassing technical SEO, content creation and link building – even if your in-house team doesn’t specialise in every area. This ensures no client gets left behind with complex needs, leading to new client acquisition, satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Competitiveness: Harness the combined power of your brand and external SEO expertise. White label partners are constantly innovating and adapting to the ever-shifting landscape, keeping you at the forefront of search engine optimization.
  • Zero Internal Scrambling: No more scrambling to build an in-house SEO team from scratch or frantically searching for niche specialists. With an SEO partner, you get instant access to a pool of seasoned SEO professionals, ready to tackle any challenge your clients throw your way.

This grants you the power to deliver exceptional results while maintaining complete control over the client experience. Your brand remains the face of the operation, building trust and fostering deeper relationships, while your SEO partners support ensures you consistently deliver a great SEO service to your clientbase.

How Agencies Can Benefit By Partnering With a White Label SEO Specialist

While most white label SEO agencies offer general advantages like expanding service portfolios and staying competitive, based on our decade of experience working with agencies and partners alike, we recommend that you also look out for key differentiators which include: 

Focus on Partnerships

Your SEO partner should build strong, collaborative relationships with agencies, seeing them as true extensions of their own team. Looking for a partner with a collaborative approach fosters trust and transparency, ensuring your agency has a say at all stages of the process, from initial strategy and KPI setting to running a campaign and reporting methods. 

Flexibility and Customisation

No two agencies are alike.  So our advice would be to make sure that the agency you partner with has a program that is highly customisable. They should offer various reporting options, including using your agency’s email address for direct client communication, and tailor their services to seamlessly align with your specific workflows, communications platforms like Slack for internal messaging and Teams or Zoom for video conferencing with clients. There’s no one-size-fits-all approach – your partner should adapt to you.

Knowledge Transfer

Your White Label SEO partner should go beyond just delivering results. They should be  dedicated to empowering your team. Check if they are willing to invest in educating your team on SEO best practices, equipping them to confidently explain the work to clients and build your own internal knowledge base. This fosters long-term partnerships, develops your internal ability to serve clients and close deals into the future.

Scalability

Agencies face many challenges including expanding offerings, adapting to diverse clients, and delivering exceptional results, all while staying flexible and cost-effective. White Label SEO solutions should address these very concerns, empowering you to scale efficiently and adapt seamlessly to client needs.

  • Expansion: White Label SEO expertise acts as your instant resource pool, enabling you to effortlessly add SEO to your service portfolio and accommodate client growth without breaking the bank.
  • No Overhead Burden: Focus your investments on areas that drive growth. Say goodbye to the overhead costs of hiring and training in-house SEO specialists. A typical minimum viable SEO team with a Head of SEO, Content writer, Off-site specialist and Technical SEO specialist would be an investment of $300,000 annually. Partnering with a specialist white label agency can offer you the chance to integrate the capability of an SEO team with virtually no upfront costs, allowing you to experiment and expand risk free. 
  • Agile Resource Allocation: Does peak season have you scrambling? White Label scales with your demands. Easily adjust your partnership to ramp up SEO support during high-pressure periods and optimise resource allocation during quieter times.
  • Streamlined Onboarding: New clients, no problem. Your SEO white label partner should offer flexible services that lets you onboard clients quickly and efficiently, focusing your energy on building strong relationships. 
  • Bespoke Reporting: Your clients deserve reports that resonate. Your SEO partner should craft customised reports tailored to your preferences, with data visualisations, brand elements, and insights that speak directly to your clients’ needs.
  • Tailored Campaign Strategies: The best SEO White Label partners  work with you to create custom campaigns for each client, targeting specific keywords, competitor analysis, and content strategies for laser-focused results.

Cost Efficient

There are two main ways that partnering with a white label SEO specialist can help you become more cost effective with your offerings.

  • Pay-as-you-go: Some SEO resellers operate on a rolling monthly fee, allowing your clients to scale their SEO efforts as they need without fixed overhead costs.
  • Competitive Pricing: White labelling might enable you to offer competitive SEO services to your clients by benefiting from their economies of scale and established processes. This can attract more clients and boost your revenue.

Broaden Your Service Offering

With an SEO white label partnership you can quickly add new services with no upfront costs or risks to building an SEO team from scratch. Your clients would benefit from: 

  • Increased website traffic and visibility: Improved search engine ranking leads to more organic website visitors, potential leads, and ultimately, revenue growth for your clients.
  • Brand reputation and authority: High search ranking builds trust and establishes your clients as thought leaders in their industry.
  • Data-driven insights and reporting: You can provide clients with detailed reports on SEO performance, demonstrating the impact of your efforts and guiding future strategies.

And SUSO can add further value with options like these: 

  • Custom SEO packages: Tailor SEO services to each client’s unique needs, goals and budgets, showcasing your ability to understand their business objectives.
  • Strategic content marketing: SEO attracts qualified leads based on search intent, combine this with your current content plans to drive on-site conversions

Dedicated Team

Consider whether your partner is able to provide a dedicated team to work on your projects. For example, each of our campaigns are made up of:

  • Account Manager: A single point of contact ensures smooth communication and project oversight, tailored to your specific needs and expectations.
  • Technical Manager: A dedicated SEO expert focused on technical aspects like website audits, on-page optimisation, and schema markup, maximising technical SEO performance.
  • Content Specialist: A skilled writer who crafts SEO-optimised content plans that will help reach your target audience and build relevance for important keywords among search engines.
  • Link Building Specialist: A specialist that secures high-quality backlinks from relevant websites, boosting your client’s domain authority and ranking.

Typically, if there’s only one point of contact who manages all aspects internally. This can lead to:

  • Lack of specialisation: One person might not have the in-depth knowledge required for each SEO component, potentially impacting results.
  • Communication bottlenecks: Delays and misunderstandings can occur as information filters through multiple team members.
  • Limited customisation: Your agency might have less control over the project and struggle to tailor it to your specific needs and brand.

Sales Support

The best SEO white labelers will help you craft winning proposals that are backed by SEO data, nurture sequences such as webinars and offer training for your team to sell the service. This would include: 

  • Training your sales team to communicate SEO confidently through in person workshops 
  • If a client is hesitant about the cost of SEO, you can present a webinar hosted by your white label partner to explain the long-term return on investment of SEO.They may even do these sessions live over a video call. 
  • Providing your clients data backed projections of keyword rankings. 
  • Sharing data driven pitch decks that show expertise and provide tangible evidence for the proposal. 

SUSO also provides free SEO insights reports which can report your clients SEO performance, highlight opportunities and outline a strategy they can evaluate for ROI. 

We also provide these insights reports in the sales cycle which: 

  • Demonstrate expertise and value: By presenting a detailed report with actionable insights, we are able to show clients the benefits that SEO can bring if incorporated into their marketing efforts. 
  • Initiate SEO conversations: The insights report becomes a conversation starter with potential clients. We use the report to discuss the website’s current SEO standing, explain the impact of the identified issues, and propose tailored solutions to address them.
  • Personalise client proposals: Each report is customised to the specific website and industry, allowing us to tailor proposals and pricing to the client’s unique needs and budget. This personalised approach can make our partner’s offer more attractive and relevant.
  • Quantify potential results: The report can go beyond just highlighting problems; it can also project potential improvements in organic traffic, rankings, and ultimately, conversions. This helps clients visualise the return on investment they can expect from SEO services.
  • Streamline the sales process: The report provides a common ground for discussion, allowing it to be focused on the client’s specific needs and concerns. This can lead to more efficient and productive sales conversations.

Focus on Core Competencies

As an agency, your strength might lie in areas like client relationship management, branding, or digital strategy. White label SEO lets you leverage specialised SEO expertise, freeing you to focus on your core competencies and excel in those areas.

An SEO white label partner handles all the technical SEO aspects, from audits and optimisation to content creation and link building. This allows you to dedicate your resources to what you do best, serving your clients and growing your agency.

There is also no need to worry about hiring, training, or managing SEO specialists. Your partner agency’s dedicated team takes care of everything, reducing your operational overhead and complexity. This streamlined approach lets you focus on strategic initiatives and client satisfaction.

Comprehensive Reporting

Crystal-clear communication and in-depth reporting form the bedrock of a successful white label SEO collaboration. For example, we centralise the entire journey under a comprehensive dashboard with live updates of the latest traffic and conversion statistics. No more scattered data or cryptic metrics – everything you and your client need sits in one place. 

Industry Connections & SEO Tool Suites

Typically, an experienced white labeller boasts strong relationships with key players in the SEO industry, granting them access to valuable resources and insights that you otherwise may not be able to access. These connections can benefit your clients in several ways:

  • Early access to industry trends and updates: They keep you ahead of the curve by being plugged into the latest SEO developments and algorithm changes. This knowledge translates into effective strategies for your clients, ensuring they stay competitive in the ever-evolving search landscape.
  • Partnerships with leading technology providers: Your white label partner will  collaborate with top SEO tool providers, gaining exclusive access to advanced features and functionalities. This allows us to deliver more comprehensive and data-driven SEO campaigns for your clients.
  • Networking opportunities and influencer relationships: industry connections open doors to valuable networking opportunities and partnerships. This can benefit your clients by increasing brand awareness, attracting potential leads, and fostering industry collaborations.

Due to economies of scale, your white label partner can leverage a powerful suite of SEO tools to optimise client websites and track campaign performance. These tools provide valuable insights and data that drive SEO strategies.

Brand Enhancement and Credibility

White label SEO can significantly contribute to your agency’s overall brand image and credibility in several ways, particularly by enabling you to offer a full suite of services to your clients:

  • By offering SEO alongside your existing services, you can cater to your clients’ diverse needs with a single point of contact. This reinforces your value proposition and positions you as a trusted provider of holistic marketing solutions.
  • By optimising your clients websites for search engines, you not only drive website traffic but also generate qualified leads and boost conversions. This directly translates to client success and increased return on investment for their marketing efforts, leading to higher satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Successfully integrating SEO into your service portfolio may also attract recognition from industry peers and award bodies – remember, we stay behind the scenes. This reinforces your brand awareness as a leading player in the digital marketing landscape.

The Bottom Line

White Label SEO solutions unlock strategic advantages for agencies, empowering them to deliver exceptional results, expand service offerings, and stay ahead of the curve.

Through partnerships with expert providers agencies can:

  • Offer comprehensive SEO services (content, technical SEO, link building) without building an in-house team.
  • Boost client satisfaction and loyalty by achieving superior results and catering to diverse needs.
  • Enhance brand image and credibility as a holistic marketing solutions provider.
  • Increase competitiveness by leveraging cutting-edge expertise and industry connections.
  • Scale efficiently and adapt seamlessly to client demands with flexible, pay-as-you-go solutions.

In addition, SUSO goes beyond by providing you with:

  • Sales support: Help with crafting proposals, training sales teams, and providing SEO data-driven projections.
  • Comprehensive reporting: Crystal-clear dashboards, regular video reports, and dedicated account manager support.
  • SUSO Partner Club: Educational content, direct Q&A access, and a supportive community for continuous learning.

By partnering with the right White Label SEO provider, agencies can unlock significant growth potential and deliver exceptional value to their clients.
